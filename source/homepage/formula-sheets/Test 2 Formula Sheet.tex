\UL{Operators}:
$\left. \, \right.$ $\nabla g(x,y,z) = [g_x,g_y,g_z]$ \hspace{1.0cm} div ${\bf F} = M_x + N_y + P_z$ \hspace{1.0cm} curl ${\bf F} = [P_y-N_z,M_z-P_x,N_x-M_y]$

\vspace{0.2cm}

\setlength{\extrarowheight}{0.2cm}
\begin{tabular}{p{9.5cm}p{9.5cm}}

\end{tabular}

	{\it Vector-Valued}: \,\,$\D{\FLUXX = \FLUXXX}$ &

\end{tabular}

\vspace{0.1cm}

\UL{Stokes' Theorem}:  Let $C$ be a closed curve which is the boundary for a surface $S$.  Then
$$
\OWORK = \STOKECURL
$$
* Recall: Green's Theorem is a special case of Stokes' Theorem where $S$ is in the $xy$-plane and ${\bf n} = [0,0,1]$.

\vspace{0.1cm}

\UL{Divergence Theorem}: Let $S$ be a surface that is the boundary of $T$. Then
$$
\FLUX = \DIVTHM
$$

\vspace{0.1cm}

\UL{Complex Numbers}:

Let $z = re^{\theta i} = r(\cos \theta + i\sin \theta)$

\qquad \, $\D{z^n = r^ne^{n\theta i} = r^n\left[\cos (n\theta) + i\sin (n\theta)\right]}$

\qquad \, $\D{z^{1/n} = r^{1/n}e^{\frac{\theta+2\pi k}{n}i} = r^{1/n}\left[\cos \left(\frac{\theta+2\pi k}{n}\right) + i\sin \left(\frac{\theta+2\pi k}{n}\right)\right]}$ \quad for \,\,$k=0,1,2,...,n-1$
