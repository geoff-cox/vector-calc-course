		 The Fourier series of a function, \,$f(x)$, \,with period, $2L$, is given by \\
		 \\
		 \hspace{2.0cm} $\D{f(x) = a_0 + \sum \limits_{n=1}^{\infty} \left[a_n\cos\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right) + b_n \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right)\right]}$ \\
		 where  \\
		 \qquad $\D{a_0 = \frac{1}{2L}\int \limits_{-L}^{L} f(x)\, dx}$ \\
		 \qquad $\D{a_n = \frac{1}{L}\int \limits_{-L}^{L} f(x) \cos\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right)\, dx}$ \\
		 \qquad $\D{b_n = \frac{1}{L}\int \limits_{-L}^{L} f(x) \sin\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right)\, dx}$ \\
