{\bf Problem \arabic{Prob}:} Let ${\bf F} = [-y,x]$ and $C$ be a simple closed curve in the $xy-$plane. Let $R$ be the region inside $C$.
 
(a) Show that $\D{\text{Area}(R) = \frac{1}{2}\oint \limits_{C} \text{\bf F} \cdot d\text{\bf r}}$

(b) Use part (a) to find the area inside the ellipse $C$: $4x^2 + y^2 = 4$ \,\,({\em Do NOT use a double integral to get the area})
