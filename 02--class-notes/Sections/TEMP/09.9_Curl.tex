\begin{frame}
\frametitle{I. Integration by Parts (IBP)}
\scriptsize
\vspace{0.2cm}

The only integration technique we have learned so far is the U-Substitution method.  \\

\vspace{0.8cm}

In the next few weeks, we will learn a number of new integration techniques and today we consider the technique known as \\

\vspace{1.2cm}

\begin{columns}\fcolorbox{blue}{white}{\begin{column}{9.4cm}
\alert{Integration by Parts}
 
% Type the contents of the box here
$$
\int u \,dv = uv - \int v \,du
$$

\vspace{0.2cm}

\end{column}}\begin{column}{0.0cm}\end{column}\end{columns}

\vspace{2.2cm}
\end{frame}

%~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
\begin{frame}
\frametitle{}
\scriptsize
\begin{columns}
\begin{column}{12.0cm}
{\bf Example}: Integrate
$$
\int x \cos x \, dx
$$

\begin{columns}\fcolorbox{blue}{white}{\begin{column}{9.4cm}
% Type the contents of the box here
\underline{Integration by Parts}:
$$
\int \textcolor<2->{blue}{{\bf u}} \textcolor<3->{RedOrange}{\,{\bf dv}} = \textcolor<6->{blue}{{\bf u}}\textcolor<7->{Emerald}{{\bf v}} - \int \textcolor<8->{Emerald}{{\bf v}} \,\textcolor<9->{Magenta}{{\bf du}}
$$
\end{column}}\begin{column}{0.0cm}\end{column}\end{columns}
\begin{columns}
\begin{column}{6.2cm}
\begin{align*}
\US{\only<10->{\text{Don't know antiderivtive}}}{\only<10->{\UB}{\int \textcolor<2->{blue}{\OS{\only<2->{\text{u}}}{\only<2->{\OB}{{\bf x}}}} 
\textcolor<3->{RedOrange}{\OS{\only<3->{\text{dv}}}{\only<3->{\OB}{{\bf \, cos \,x \, dx}}}}}} \, & 
\onslide<6->{= \textcolor{blue}{\OS{\only<6->{\text{u}}}{\only<6->{\OB}{{\bf x}}}} 
\onslide<7->{\textcolor{Emerald}{\OS{\only<7->{\text{v}}}{\only<7->{\OB}{{\bf \,sin \,x}}}}} }
\ON<8->{\,\, -}  
\US{\only<10->{\text{Know antiderivtive}}}{\only<10->{\UB}{\onslide<8->{\int \textcolor{Emerald}{\OS{\text{v}}{\OB{{\bf  \,sin \,x}}}}} \, 
\onslide<9->{\textcolor{Magenta}{\OS{\text{du}}{\OB{{\bf dx}}}}}}} \\
&\\
\onslide<11->{= \, & x \sin x - (- \cos x ) + C} \\
&\\
\onslide<12->{= \, & x \sin x + \cos x + C} \\
\end{align*}

\end{column}
\begin{column}{4.2cm}
\vspace{0.2cm}

\onslide<2->{Choose \textcolor<2->{blue}{{\bf u}} \onslide<3->{, \textcolor<3->{RedOrange}{{\bf dv}} is what is left over.}
\onslide<4->{Calculate \onslide<4->{\textcolor<4->{Magenta}{{\bf du}}} \onslide<5->{and \textcolor<5->{Emerald}{{\bf v}}.}}}
$$
\begin{array}{|p{2.0cm} p{2.0cm}}
\hline
\onslide<2->{\textcolor<2->{blue}{{\bf u = x}}} & 
\onslide<5->{\textcolor<5->{Emerald}{{\bf v = \,sin \,x}}} \\
& \\
\onslide<4->{\textcolor<4->{Magenta}{{\bf du = dx}}} & 
\onslide<3->{\textcolor<3->{RedOrange}{{\bf  dv = \,cos \,x \, dx}}}\\
\hline
\end{array}
$$

\vspace{2.0cm}
\end{column}
\end{columns}

\begin{frame}
\frametitle{II. Strategy for Choosing $u$}
\scriptsize
\begin{columns}
\begin{column}{12.0cm}
Since the success of using integration by parts depends on the $u$ you choose, then we will briefly cover a common strategy for choosing $u$. \\

\vspace{0.6cm}

Note: Once $u$ is chosen, $du$, $dv$, and $v$ are determined \\

\vspace{0.6cm}

The order in which you should choose $u$ is as follows: \\

\vspace{0.5cm}

%-------LIST---------
\begin{itemize}
	\item {\bf L}: {\bf L}ogarithms \qquad - \qquad Example: \quad \ON<2->{\B{$\ln x$}}
	\vspace{0.2cm}
	\item {\bf I}: {\bf I}nverse Trig \qquad - \qquad Example: \quad \ON<3->{\B{$\arccos x$ or $\tan^{-1} t$}}
	\vspace{0.2cm}
	\item {\bf A}: {\bf A}lgebraic \qquad \,\, - \qquad Example: \quad \ON<4->{\B{$x$, $x^2$, or $x^3$}}
	\vspace{0.2cm}
	\item {\bf T}: {\bf T}rig \qquad \hspace{0.8cm} - \qquad Example: \quad \ON<5->{\B{$\sin 3x$}}
	\vspace{0.2cm}
	\item {\bf E}: {\bf E}xponential \qquad - \qquad Example: \quad \ON<6->{\B{$e^{2x}$ or $2^x$}}
\end{itemize}
%-------------------

\vspace{0.2cm}
	
\end{column}

\begin{align*}
	\int_{1}^{4} \only<2->{\OS{\B{\text{u}}}}{\only<2->{\OB}{ x }} 
		 \only<2->{\OS{\B{\text{dv}}}}{\only<2->{\OB}{ f''(x) \, dx }} = 
		 &\only<1-5>{\hspace{1.5cm}}
		 \ON<6->{\only<6->{\OS{\B{\text{u}}}}{\only<6->{\OB}{ \B{ (x) }} }} 
		 \ON<6->{\only<6->{\OS{\B{\text{v}}}}{\only<6->{\OB}{ \B{ (f'(x)) }} }}
		 \ON<6->{\B{\,\, -\int\limits_{1}^{4} }}
		 \ON<6->{\only<6->{\OS{\B{\text{v}}}}{\only<6->{\OB}{ \B{ (f'(x)) }} }} 
		 \ON<6->{\only<6->{\OS{\B{\text{du}}}}{\only<6->{\OB}{ \B{ (dx) }} }} \\
		 &\ON<7->{\B{= \left.xf'(x)\right|_{1}^{4} - \int \limits_{1}^{4} f'(x)\, dx}} \\
		 &\ON<8->{\B{= \left.xf'(x) - f(x)\right|_{1}^{4}}} \\
		 &\ON<9->{\B{= [(4)f'(4) - f(4)] - [(1)f'(1) - f(1)]}} \\
		 &\ON<10->{\B{= [4(3) - 7] - [5 - 2]}} \\
		 &\ON<11->{\B{= 5 - 3 = 2}} \\
\end{align*}
