{In general, explain one relationship between secant lines and tangent lines on a function $f(x)$ (in the context of this section).}
{Secant lines can be used to estimate tangent lines.  For example, to find a tangent line through the point $(6,f(6))$, one could instead find the secant line through points $(6,f(6))$ and $(6.0001,f(6.0001))$.  The same technique is used to estimate the slope of a tangent line.}
