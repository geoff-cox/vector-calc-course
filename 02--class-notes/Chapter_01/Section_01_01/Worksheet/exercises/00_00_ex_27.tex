{Suppose an object's position function is given by $s(t)$.  Explain how average velocity could be used to estimate the velocity of an object after $6$s.}
{Since velocity is the slope of the tangent line of $s(t)$ at $6$s and average velocity is the slope of a secant line of $s(t)$, then just as with the previous problem, the velocity of the object at $t=6$ could be estimated using the average velocity over the time interval $[6,6.0001]$.}
